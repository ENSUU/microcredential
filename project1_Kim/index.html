About Indonesia:

Indonesia is a country located off the coast of mainland Southeast Asia in the Indian and Pacific oceans. Indonesia is an archipelago, made up of more than 17,000 islands! Indonesia is surrounded by Malaysia, the Phillippines, Papua New Guinea, and Australia.

In the early 21st century, Indonesia was the most populous country in Southeast Asia and the fourth most populous in the world!
